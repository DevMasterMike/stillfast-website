What it does

Built for the split second

Reaction time decides the first step, the save, the read. Stillfast makes warming it up a habit.

One tap, a new warm-up every time

One tap starts a short set of reaction drills — different every time, so your brain never settles into a pattern. Plus an endless Survival mode when you want to chase a score.

See your reaction numbers, session over session

Every drill is logged. Labs leads with your numbers — fastest reaction, weekly average, the trend line. Tap any drill for its full history, or export a clean report for your coach.

Your whole library, tuned to your sport

Every drill in one place. Filter to your sport, set your level or let a quick test find it, and warm up your game reading — or drop into Survival. Search it, reorder it, make it yours.

Simple Reaction, Go / No-Go, Peripheral Catch, Object Tracking, and more — across speed, choice, vision, tracking, memory, and sound. None play the same way twice.
