What it does

Train the moment before the moment

Reaction time decides the first step, the save, the read. Stillfast makes warming it up a habit.

One tap, a new warm-up every time

A short set of reaction drills, reshuffled and re-seeded on every launch so your brain can never settle into a pattern. Over twenty drills across speed, choice, peripheral vision, tracking, memory, and sound, plus an endless Survival challenge when you want to chase a score.

See your reaction numbers, session over session

Every drill is logged. The Labs tab leads with your numbers, a lab readout of your fastest reaction, sessions, and drills trained, then your weekly average and whether the number is trending up or down, with a tap into any drill for its full trend. Export a clean report to share with a coach or team, so your trend is a number you can actually watch, not a feeling.

Your whole library, tuned to your sport

The Train tab holds every drill in one place. Filter to your sport for the ones whose demands resemble how you compete, set the difficulty or let a quick test find your level, warm up your game reading, or jump into the endless Survival challenge. Search it, reorder it, make it yours.

Built around how you compete

Everything in its place

Over twenty reaction drills

Simple Reaction, Go / No-Go, Peripheral Catch, Object Tracking, Chase Light, Anticipation, and many more, across speed, choice, peripheral vision, tracking, memory, and sound. None of them play the same way twice.

Your sport, or not

Pick from a dozen sports and Stillfast suggests drills whose demands resemble how you compete, or choose Everyday for general mental sharpness if you are not into sports. Override anytime.

Survival mode

An endless reaction challenge that ramps up until you miss. Chase your best score, then beat it.

Playlists and an exportable lab report

Save favorite drills into playlists and pin them to your home screen. Every result is logged, and you can export a clean PDF report to hand a coach.

Never the same twice

Every warm-up, every playlist, every drill is reshuffled and re-seeded each run. Predictability is the enemy of reaction training, so Stillfast designs it out. Link Apple Calendar and it even reminds you to warm up before each game.

∞

Yours, and only yours

Your profile, drills, and results live on your device and sync through your own private iCloud account. We do not run servers that receive your data, there is no advertising, and there is no third-party tracking. Export or delete everything from inside the app at any time.
